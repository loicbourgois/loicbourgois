The chicken eggs-change is an exchange where people can buy right to consume eggs in order to help fund chicken retirement.This exchange allows people to pay for eggs ahead of time so that chicken farmers will be able to care for chickens in retirement and help create a better future for chickens. The exchange has been designed to help reduce the amount of chickens in factory farms and help bring about humane and ethical practices for egg-laying hens. People who purchase eggs through the exchange can rest assured that the chickens producing the eggs are treated in a humane and ethical way. The eggs are also healthier since the chickens producing them are not subjected to the same conditions that chickens in factory farms face.
